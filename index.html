DayZ Academy

Tips & Tricks

01

Never trust green smoke.

It marks a route, not safety. Scout hills, roofs, and treelines before moving in.

02

Carry medicine before PvP.

Vitamins, tetra, charcoal, bandages, and splints save runs more than extra loot.

03

Travel light to trader routes.

Heavy bags slow you down and turn every open field into a gamble.

04

Use overwatch.

One player watches while the other approaches. Solo players should move slow and quiet.
